Saturn




  • Saturn is known for its stunning ring system.

    Saturn's ring system is composed of icy particles, rocks, and dust. It is the most extensive and elaborate ring system of any planet in our solar system.

  • Saturn is a gas giant with a relatively low density.

    Despite its large size, Saturn is less dense than water. If you could find a bathtub large enough to hold Saturn, the planet would float!

  • Saturn has a hexagonal-shaped storm at its north pole.

    This mysterious hexagon is a persistent cloud pattern and is one of the most striking features of the planet. The hexagon's exact cause and dynamics are still under investigation.

  • Saturn has numerous moons, including Titan.

    Titan is Saturn's largest moon and is known for having a thick atmosphere. It's the only moon in our solar system with a substantial atmosphere, and it's larger than the planet Mercury.

  • Saturn has a relatively short day and a long year.

    Saturn's day lasts about 10.7 hours, making it one of the fastest-spinning planets. However, it takes approximately 29.5 Earth years for Saturn to complete one orbit around the Sun.
